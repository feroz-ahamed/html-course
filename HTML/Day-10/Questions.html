Block element occupies full width of the screen and it always starts in a new line.

What is d/f b/w the semantic elements and non-semantic elements?

The difference between semantic elements and non-semantic elements is:
Semantic elements clearly describe their meaning to both the browser and the developer. They provide structure to the content, making it easier for search engines to understand the content's purpose.
Examples: header,article,nav,section,footer.
Non-Semantic elements do not convey any meaning about the content within them. They are more about presentation and layout rather than structure.
Examples: div,span
